About Maple Garden

The story of a garden room, a diploma in stillness, and a therapist who truly understands what it means to need to switch off.

Andrea’s Story

Finding calm through the hardest of times

In 2021, 31 weeks into her pregnancy, Andrea McConville was diagnosed with triple negative breast cancer. She underwent chemotherapy, surgery and radiotherapy while caring for her newborn son, Freddie — one of the most demanding periods anyone could face.

Throughout her treatment, meditation became her anchor. It was the tool she turned to whenever anxiety crept in, or when a particularly difficult day lay ahead. That experience didn’t just help her through — it changed the direction of her life.

Determined to understand meditation more deeply and share its benefits with others, Andrea trained formally as a meditation teacher and therapist. Maple Garden Skincare, Meditation & Wellbeing was born from that journey: a private garden room in Urmston where massage, skincare and mindful breathing come together to help others find the same sense of calm that carried her through.

“Meditation was my go-to tool which I relied on when I felt anxious or had to face a challenging time during my cancer treatment.”
