About Maple Garden

The story of a garden room, a diploma in stillness, and a therapist who truly understands what it means to need to switch off.

My Story

Finding calm through the hardest of times

In 2021, 31 weeks into my pregnancy, I was diagnosed with triple negative breast cancer. I underwent chemotherapy, surgery and radiotherapy while caring for my newborn son, Freddie — one of the most demanding periods I’ve ever faced.

Throughout my treatment, meditation became my anchor. It was the tool I turned to whenever anxiety crept in, or when a particularly difficult day lay ahead. That experience didn’t just help me through — it changed the direction of my life.

Determined to understand meditation more deeply and share its benefits with others, I trained formally as a meditation teacher and therapist. Maple Garden Skincare, Meditation & Wellbeing was born from that journey: a private garden room in Urmston where massage, skincare and mindful breathing come together to help others find the same sense of calm that carried me through.

“Meditation was my go-to tool which I relied on when I felt anxious or had to face a challenging time during my cancer treatment.”
